    It's not easy but I prospered

    The good things

    I joined when JobStreet.com was way behind the newspapers. At a time when every Saturday, one newspaper alone has 180 pages of job adverts. It was extremely tough selling against them but the management had the vision and my managers managed to keep us motivated enough to keep pushing. Today, we are the one being targeted to be 'replaced.' I think, I will just do my best to keep contributing.

    The challenges

    The world is changing and the competition is intensifying. It's now even more important to get back that fighting spirit and not rest on our laurels. Perhaps it's time to be tougher on those who are not ready instead of giving chances for the staffs to slowly adapt / change. Truth is, if we are not good here, we should find another place when we are better. :)

    Company Overview
    JobStreet.com has been the pioneer Online Job Site in Asia Pacific since 1995 and today we operate in eight countries.  We have been helping companies, big and small,  meet their talent needs by providing a comprehensive suite of talent sourcing, employer branding and talent management solutions.
    Why join us?
    Most companies are about profit.  JobStreet.com is about  helping society find employment.  We believe we can make the world a better place, one job at a time.  What does this mean?  It’s simple…better jobs mean better lives.  It’s why we started this company and it remains our core principle today.
     
    We believe the best employee is not just the smartest and brightest.  Instead, it’s the people who share our passion, commitment and obsession in carrying out our mission of improving lives through better careers.
     
    Join our team and help us develop new and innovative products and services in order to improve the lives of others, and the way people will be hired in the future.
